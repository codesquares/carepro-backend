# CarePro — Gig Deletion & Data Retention Policy

**Effective Date:** March 15, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

---

## 1. Overview

This policy explains what happens when you delete a gig on CarePro, how long we keep your data, your right to restore deleted gigs, and how we handle your personal information in compliance with data protection regulations including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR).

---

## 2. What Happens When You Delete a Gig

When you choose to delete a gig from your CarePro profile:

- **Your gig is immediately hidden** from all search results, public profiles, and client-facing pages. No one will be able to see or book the deleted gig.
- **Your gig data is preserved** for a 30-day grace period in case you change your mind.
- **You will receive confirmation** that your gig has been successfully deleted.

### What You Cannot Delete

To protect both you and your clients, a gig **cannot be deleted** if it currently has:

- **Active contracts** — Complete or terminate all contracts before deleting the gig.
- **Active subscriptions** — Cancel all recurring subscriptions tied to the gig first.
- **In-progress orders** — All orders must be completed or resolved before deletion.

If any of these conditions exist, you will receive a clear message explaining what needs to be resolved first.

---

## 3. 30-Day Grace Period

After deletion, you have **30 calendar days** to restore your gig.

### How to Restore

You can restore a deleted gig through the CarePro app or by contacting our support team. When restored:

- Your gig will be placed in **Draft status** — it will not be immediately visible to clients.
- You should **review your gig details** (pricing, description, availability) before republishing, as information may have become outdated.
- Once you are satisfied, you can republish the gig to make it active again.

### After 30 Days

Once the 30-day grace period expires:

- **Your gig is permanently deleted** and cannot be recovered.
- Certain related data is anonymized or removed as described in Section 4 below.
- This action is **irreversible**. Please be certain before letting the grace period expire.

---

## 4. What Data We Keep and Why

After the 30-day grace period, we process your gig-related data as follows:

| Data Type | What Happens | Why |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| **Gig listing** (title, description, images, pricing) | Permanently deleted | No longer needed |
| **Visit check-in locations** (GPS data) | Permanently deleted | Sensitive personal data with no retention need |
| **Notifications** related to the gig | Permanently deleted | No retention value |
| **Pending payments & booking commitments** | Permanently deleted | Operational data, no longer relevant |
| **Reviews** | Anonymized (reviewer identity removed, rating kept) | Platform quality metrics |
| **Contracts** | Anonymized (personal addresses, access instructions removed; financial terms kept) | Financial audit trail |
| **Task sheets** | Anonymized (signatures removed, task structure kept) | Service analytics |
| **Observation reports** | Anonymized (descriptions redacted, photos removed) | Category and severity tracking |
| **Billing records** | Retained for **7 years** | Required by Nigerian tax law and financial regulations |
| **Incident reports** | Retained until legal retention period expires | Legal liability and safety compliance |
| **Order history** | Retained for financial audit | Tax and regulatory compliance |

## 5. Your Data Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

### Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)
You may request the deletion of all your personal data held by CarePro. We will process your request within **30 days**. Note that some data may be retained where we have a legal obligation to do so (e.g., financial records required by tax authorities).

### Right to Access
You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### Right to Rectification
You may request correction of any inaccurate personal data.

### Right to Data Portability
You may request your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address provided in Section 7.

---

## 6. Account Deletion

If you choose to delete your entire CarePro account:

- **All your gigs** will follow the deletion process described above.
- **All personal data** tied to your account will be deleted or anonymized after the grace period, except where legal retention requirements apply.
- Financial records (billing, payments, tax-relevant data) are retained for the legally mandated period.
